About Us

Welcome to our artistic journey! Andrea Mae Sinsay and Edmar Gontiñas are both passionate Filipino artist driven by a deep love for capturing the beauty of our culture and nature through our art. Growing up in Cavite, We have always been inspired by the vibrant colors of our landscapes, the rich tapestry of our traditions, and the resilience of our people.

Our artistic style is a blend of modern interpretations infused with Filipino motifs and symbolism. Through our paintings, I strive to evoke emotions, tell stories, and celebrate the essence of Filipino identity. Each brushstroke is a reflection of our heritage and a homage to the diversity that defines us.
